OUR STORY

As told by Matt

Sally swiped left

Like all good love stories, Sally saw Matt's profile on a dating app... then rejected him. Two days later, they met over dinner with a mutual friend. Turns out, he's much better looking in person.

Matt began finding excuses to spend time with Sally, even staying up past 9 PM to see a DJ at a club. That night, he told her he wanted to date her. She responded by taking a shot and dragging him to the dance floor. The rest is history.

Matt & Sally roam

Since Memorial Day 2016, Matt and Sally have had epic adventures together -- from camping in the Sierras, sailing the Caribbean, to weekends in Mexico. A near-psychotic tendency to plan keeps these two on the go.

In between, Matt introduced Sally to things like regular sleep and adequate protein. In return, Sally taught Matt, to his disbelief, that managing your personal finances is easier than CrossFit.

Matt put a ring on it

Matt knew he would marry Sally from the 1st time they went out. This gave him hitherto unseen drive to hit his bonuses at work, so he could buy a relatively un-conflicted diamond.

After their marathon, Matt planned a surprise getaway in SoCal. After brunch and a spa day, he brought her to the cutest Airstream trailer on the beach. Later that night, he proposed... and they immediately began planning the wedding.

Thanks to Clara Rice Photography for our awesome engagement shoot!
